Our Menu

Welcome to our Menu, where every dish tells a story of flavor, passion, and culinary craftsmanship. Our selections are more than just meals; they are experiences waiting to be savored, fully customizable to suit your unique taste. Below, we are thrilled to showcase a few of our specialty dishes, each with their own distinct flair and a range of delectable variations.

Lobster Delicacy

Experience the luxurious blend of fresh lobster on a crispy flatbread, enhanced with a touch of gourmet cheese and herbs.

Alternative Variations:

  • Garlic Butter Lobster: Drenched in a homemade garlic butter, offering a rich and savory taste.
  • Chili Lime Lobster: Marinated with chili and lime for a zesty flavor kick.
  • Herb Grilled Lobster: Grilled with a mix of herbs for a fragrant and flavorful experience.

Succulent Tomahawk Steak

Savor the rich flavors of our perfectly aged Tomahawk steak, grilled to perfection for a sublime dining experience.

Alternative Variations:

  • Pepper Crust Tomahawk: Encrusted with peppercorns for a spicy outer layer.
  • Bourbon Glazed Tomahawk: Glazed with bourbon sauce for a sweet and smoky flavor.
  • Herb Butter Tomahawk: Topped with a dollop of herb-infused butter for added richness.

Golden Fried Catfish

Delight in the classic taste of Southern-style catfish, fried to crispy perfection and served with a side of our signature sauce.

Alternative Variations:

  • Lemon Herb Catfish: Seasoned with lemon and herbs for a light and refreshing flavor.
  • Cajun Spiced Catfish: Coated in Cajun spices for a hot and spicy kick.
  • Smoked Paprika Catfish: Rubbed with smoked paprika for a subtle smokiness.

Ultimate Smoked Burgers

Indulge in our smoked burgers, offering a unique blend of smoky flavors and juicy beef, topped with fresh, artisanal ingredients.

Alternative Variations:

  • Chipotle BBQ Burger: Topped with chipotle BBQ sauce for a tangy and smoky taste.
  • Blue Cheese Burger: With crumbled blue cheese for a sharp and creamy flavor.
  • Mushroom Swiss Burger: Loaded with sautéed mushrooms and Swiss cheese

Specialty Seasoned Chicken

Explore our diverse range of expertly seasoned chicken dishes, each crafted to bring unique flavors and culinary experiences. From our signature Zesty Smoked Cilantro Lime Chicken to a variety of other seasoned selections, we cater to every taste with customizable variations.

Zesty Smoked Cilantro Lime Chicken: This signature dish features tender chicken marinated in a vibrant cilantro and lime blend, then smoked to perfection. It's a flavorful journey that highlights our dedication to culinary innovation and quality.

Custom variations available upon request. Let us craft a seasoned chicken dish that perfectly matches your event's theme and your guests' palates.

  • Herb-Infused Roast Chicken: Slow-roasted with a medley of fresh herbs, offering a juicy and aromatic feast.
  • Spicy Buffalo Chicken: Coated in a tangy and spicy buffalo sauce, perfect for those who love a bit of heat.
  • Garlic Lemon Butter Chicken: Sauteed in a rich garlic lemon butter sauce, delivering a refreshing zest.
  • BBQ Glazed Chicken: Brushed with a homemade BBQ sauce for a sweet and smoky finish.
